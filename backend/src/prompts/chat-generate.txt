**SCRIPT / SHOT LIST**

- [3-8s] Next scene description
- etc.

**CAPTION**
Full caption text with emojis.

**HASHTAGS**
10-15 relevant hashtags.

**CTA**
The call-to-action (comment, save, share, follow, etc.).
